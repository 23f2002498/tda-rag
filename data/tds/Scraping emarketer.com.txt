<article class="markdown-section" id="main"><h2 id="scraping-emarketer"><a class="anchor" data-id="scraping-emarketer" href="#/scraping-emarketer?id=scraping-emarketer"><span>Scraping emarketer</span></a></h2><p>In this live scraping session, we explore a real-life scenario where Straive had to scrape data from emarketer.com for a demo. This is a fairly realistic and representative way of how one might go about scraping a website.</p><p><a href="https://youtu.be/ZzUsDE1XjhE" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><img alt="Live scraping session" data-origin="https://i.ytimg.com/vi_webp/ZzUsDE1XjhE/sddefault.webp" src="https://i.ytimg.com/vi_webp/ZzUsDE1XjhE/sddefault.webp"/></a></p><p>You’ll learn:</p><ul><li><strong>Scraping</strong>: How to extract data from web pages, including constructing URLs, fetching page content, and parsing HTML using packages like <a href="https://lxml.de/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><code>lxml</code></a> and <a href="https://www.python-httpx.org/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><code>httpx</code></a>.</li><li><strong>Caching</strong>: Implementing a caching strategy to avoid redundant data fetching for efficiency and reliability.</li><li><strong>Error Handling and Debugging</strong>: Practical tips for troubleshooting, such as using liberal print statements, breakpoints for in-depth debugging, and the concept of “rubber duck debugging” to clarify problems.</li><li><strong>LLMs</strong>: Benefits of Gemini / ChatGPT for code suggestions and troubleshooting.</li><li><strong>Real-World Application</strong>: How quick proofs of concept to showcase capabilities to clients, emphasizing practice over theory.</li></ul><div class="docsify-pagination-container">
